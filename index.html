PhD Student  ·  UC Berkeley Statistics

Email GitHub Google Scholar

I am a PhD student in Statistics at UC Berkeley, advised by Song Mei and Michael I. Jordan. My research is at the intersection of statistics and LLMs, including deep learning theory, mechanistic interpretability, LLM reasoning, and data science agents. I received my B.S. from Peking University, where I worked with Peng Ding.
